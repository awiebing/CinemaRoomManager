Printing even numbers with a skip condition

Hey there! This problem might be a bit unpredictable, but give it a go and let us know how you do!
Write a program in Java that asks the user for a single input, an integer 'n'. Your task is to print the integer 'n' and then print all even integers up to 'n'. However, if the integer 10 is encountered while printing, your program should skip the number 10 and the five numbers that follow it. Then, continue printing the remaining even numbers until 'n'.
Sample Input:
20

Sample Output:
2 4 6 8 16 18 20


Sample Input:
11

Sample Output:
2 4 6 8



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 5 seconds
